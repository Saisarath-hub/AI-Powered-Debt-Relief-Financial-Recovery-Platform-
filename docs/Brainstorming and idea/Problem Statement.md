# Problem Statement

## Title

AI Powered Debt Relief and Financial Recovery Platform

## Background

Managing personal debt has become increasingly challenging due to rising living expenses, multiple loan obligations, and limited financial awareness. Many people find it difficult to evaluate their financial condition and choose appropriate repayment strategies.

## Problem

Traditional financial calculators provide only numerical outputs and do not offer personalized financial guidance. Users often struggle to understand whether their financial condition is healthy or risky.

## Proposed Solution

The proposed AI Powered Debt Relief and Financial Recovery Platform provides an intelligent solution by analyzing user financial data such as income, expenses, debt, and credit score. Based on this information, the system calculates financial metrics and generates AI-based recommendations for debt management.

## Objectives

- Analyze financial information.
- Calculate debt-to-income ratio.
- Estimate monthly EMI.
- Suggest debt settlement amount.
- Generate AI-based financial advice.
- Present financial information through charts and dashboards.

## Expected Outcome

The platform will help users better understand their financial condition, improve financial planning, reduce debt risks, and make informed decisions through AI-generated recommendations.

## Conclusion

The proposed solution provides an efficient, user-friendly, and intelligent approach to personal debt management by integrating Artificial Intelligence with modern web technologies.
